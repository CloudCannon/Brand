Emblem & Text

When using the logo the pairing with text is prefered.

Emblem Only

If space is precious or CloudCannon is prevalent in logo surroundings use the emblem only.

When the icon is used for an Icon or badge the emblem on blue should always be used.

General Rules

  • Do not distort or change the logo
  • Do not have the text on its own
  • Do not display the logo in anything but white, blacks or CloudCannon blue. Greys are acceptable.
