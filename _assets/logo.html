--- title: Logo ---
  • Do not distort or change the logo/emblem
  • Do not have the text on its own
  • Do not display the logo/emblem in anything but white, black, grey or CloudCannon blue

Emblem and Text

When using the logo the pairing with text is preferred.

  • Logo SVG
  • Blue Logo SVG

Emblem Only

If space is precious or CloudCannon is prevalent in logo surroundings use the emblem only.

When the icon is used for an Icon or badge the emblem on blue should always be used.

When spacing the emblem there should be space equal to the center circle on either side. The emblem should be vertically aligned and have a minimum size that ensures a circle would fit above and below.
