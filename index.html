Brand

Design is very important at CloudCannon. These rules and assets exist to strengthen our consistency across all sites. This website is for:

  • Internal members of the team for all new assets
  • External people referencing CloudCannon

This documentation is a work in progress. If you find inconsistencies or something is unclear please contact our support.

Assets

  • Do not distort or change the logo/emblem
  • Do not have the text on its own
  • Do not display the logo/emblem in anything but white, black, grey or CloudCannon blue

Emblem Only

If space is a premium or CloudCannon is prevalent in the logo surroundings use the emblem only.

When the emblem is used for an icon or badge, the white emblem on blue should always be used.

There should be space equal to the center circle on either side. The emblem should be vertically aligned and have a minimum size that ensures a circle would fit above and below.

  • Blue emblem: SVG
